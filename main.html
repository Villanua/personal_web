About me

Hello! I'm Nacho, a robotics engineer born in Malaga and currently residing in Madrid. My passion for robotics and programming has shaped my career. I firmly believe that robotics is poised to revolutionize the world, enhancing the quality of life in all areas. That's why I find great motivation and a sense of satisfaction in the projects I engage in.



Career

Degree in Industrial Technologies Engineering

I studied a Degree in Industrial Technologies Engineering, where I specialized in the field of electronics and automation.

In the first years I learned general engineering knowledge, finally getting into robotics with various subjects of industrial robotics, computer vision and control systems.

I finished my studies developing my Final Degree Project, called "Estrategia de colaboración humano-robot en cirugía endonasal" (Strategy of human-robot collaboration in endonasal surgery), participating in a national plan project called "Planificador global de un sistema robótico para anastomosis" (Global planner of a robotic system for anastomosis) and in the article "Collaborative Robotic Assistant Platform for Endonasal Surgery: Preliminary In-Vitro Surgery: Preliminary In-Vitro Trials".
